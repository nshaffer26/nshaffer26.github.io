Playtesting Notes:

Levels:
  • 0-10 too much
    • Change to 1-6
    • Too long of game if you have to go to addiction level 10
      • Addiction level 10 involved 100 cigarettes
  • Add consumption level
    • Need to consume cigarettes each round
      • Add withdrawal function
        • Ability to go down in addiction level
  • Adjustment of addiction and consumption level
    • Consumption is said #
      • Addiction is double the amount
  • Initially 3 withdrawal level
    • Too hard to achieve decrease addition level
    • Lower to 2 withdrawal levels
  • Drawing change
    • Levels 1-2: draw 1
    • Levels 3-4: draw 2
    • Levels 5-6: draw 3
    • Withdrawal Level 1: draw one extra
    • Withdrawal Level 2: draw two extra
Boards:
