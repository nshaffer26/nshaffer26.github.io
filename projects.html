Fractals

About:

This program is the result of a collaboration between myself and two other UB students during my freshman year at UB. In addition, it uses some classes and libraries developed by an instructor. In it, you can generate four different types of fractals and make multiple changes to the way they look and are generated. You can even zoom by clicking and dragging the mouse.

This program is a game I created for my DMS 110 Midterm Project. For this project, I decided to model my game after a brick breaking game in which there is a platform at the bottom of the screen, rows of blocks at the top of the screen, and a ball bouncing between them.
