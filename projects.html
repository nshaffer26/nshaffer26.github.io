Fractals

About:

This program is the result of a collaboration between myself and two other UB students during my first semester at UB. In addition, it uses some classes and libraries developed by an instructor. In it, you can generate four different types of fractals and make multiple changes to the way they look and are generated. You can even zoom by clicking and dragging the mouse.
