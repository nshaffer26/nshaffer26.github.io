Summary:

The inspiration for Puff Puff Pass came from the documentary Making a Killing: Philip Morris, Kraft and Global Tobacco Addiction, by Kelly Anderson and Tami Goldand. This film explores the questionable means of advertising by the tobacco industry, and how this advertising has been used to target children.

The concept for our game is to highlight this type of behavior by presenting the player as a student already addicted to smoking cigarettes. The goal of the game is for the player to try to slow their rising addiction level.

Our team: Nida Ali, John Fields, Mark Gordon, Nick Shaffer
