Noteworthy

The following technologies were deployed in the making of this project:

  • Web components were used to create custom elements. They were utilized for the website's header, footer, and navigation
  • Icons8 was used to obtain basic icons
  • Google Fonts was used to obtain a unique font for the website

My work on this project goes outside what was covered in class in several ways, including:

  • A custom font from Google Fonts
  • Four unique enemy behavior patterns:
    • Tier 1 enemies bounce around the screen and occasionally change direction
    • Tier 2 enemies chase the player
    • Tier 3 enemies chase and shoot at the player
    • Tier 4 enemies move around the screen and periodically stop to shoot at the player
  • Four unique bullet behaviors:
    • Circle bullets fly straight in the direction you were facing
    • Triangle bullets target and chase an enemy
    • Square bullets explode on impact with an enemy and send surrounding enemies away
    • Hexagon bullets implode on impact with an enemy and suck in surrounding enemies
  • The spawn rates of enemy tiers and bullet types are based on the level achieved and are read in from a JSON file
  • All bullets fire to a beat denoted by the grid below the game
  • The player can choose a new bullet (or health) at the end of each level to be added to their inventory
  • The player can move their avatar using WASD and aim with the mouse
  • Each player's best level and time is recorded to local storage and updated and displayed as they progress
